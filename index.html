TUTORIAL

You are about to take part in a chat-based negotiation between an employer and a job candidate.

You are the employer.

These are the negotiation issues

  • Salary
  • Job description
  • Car benefit
  • Pension fund
  • Promotion track
  • Working hours

Each issue has a set of possible values with corresponding scores.

Your goal is to achieve a maximal score based on a score table.

A bonus to your reward depends on the score.

Some issues contain No agreement value.

These issues are minor issues and an agreement can be reached without agreeing on them explicitly.

The maximum negotiation time is 30 minutes.

As time passes, you can achieve better agreement and earn a higher score.

Your score table can be seen by pressing Your scores button.

This is where you write your text. Write text in plain English, as if you were talking. Press Say to send the message to the other player.

The history of the actions will be presented in the above box.

To sign on an agreement you must click the same values to which your partner agreed. As you click on the same one, it turns green.

In addition Your score field shows you the total score of the chosen values.

By pressing Opt-out you end the negotiation without signing the agreement.
